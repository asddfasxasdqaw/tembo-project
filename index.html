Anatomy of a skill

Every skill is one Markdown file with four blocks. Drop it in — your model now has the spec, the rules and the deliverables, baked in.

01

Persona

Who the model is pretending to be. Years of experience, taste, the studios they've worked at.

02

Rules

The non-negotiables: tokens, naming, a11y, performance budgets, file structure.

03

References

Curated visual & code references — what good looks like, what to copy, what to avoid.

04

Deliverables

A numbered list of what must come out. The model stops only when every item is shipped.
